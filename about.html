Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Eman Fatima, a final-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) student with a strong interest in frontend web development. I enjoy designing and building responsive websites that combine clean layouts with a user-friendly experience. Every project I complete helps me strengthen my technical skills while allowing me to express creativity through web design.

I began my journey by learning the fundamentals of HTML and CSS and gradually expanded my knowledge to Bootstrap, WordPress, and SQL. I believe that continuous learning is essential in the ever-changing field of technology, so I actively explore modern web development practices and improve my skills through personal projects and online courses.

Beyond technical knowledge, I value problem-solving, attention to detail, teamwork, and effective communication. These qualities help me create websites that are not only visually appealing but also practical and accessible for users.

My long-term goal is to become a skilled Full Stack Web Developer. I am currently focused on strengthening my frontend development skills while preparing to learn JavaScript, React, Node.js, and other modern technologies that will enable me to build complete web applications from start to finish.
